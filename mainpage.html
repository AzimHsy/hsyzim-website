I also thought ,

The color of Orange,
so I could see the sunset and the autumn leaves

- Orange is a vibrant and energetic color, often associated with warmth, enthusiasm, and creativity

But then I felt like ,

I wanted to say Green!
so I could see the grass and the trees

- Green is commonly associated with nature, growth, and renewal, evoking a sense of harmony and balance.

My mind also said ,

Violet!
so I could see the flowers and tulips!

- Purple is a color rich with symbolism and history, often associated with royalty, luxury, and spirituality.

Or maybe ,

The color of Red!
so I could see the Roses and the red sky!

- Red is a powerful and intense color associated with a wide range of emotions, from love and passion to anger and urgency.
